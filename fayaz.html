About

Many customers mentioned that the shawarma at this restaurant is delicious and worth trying. Some reviews appreciated the taste of the chicken biryani and tandoori dishes. A few customers enjoyed the Fayazi chicken, describing it as soft and delicious.. One customer mentioned that the service needs improvement, specifically in terms of cleanliness and promptness.
