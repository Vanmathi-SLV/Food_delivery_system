About

Ambience is good and creative. Good variety of food and drinks. Falooda, puttu ice cream, cheesy fries, milkshakes and sandwiches are some of the recommended items. Friendly staff..Quality of some food items like French fries, falloda, and butterscotch bomb is not up to the mark. Limited seating arrangements and small parking area.
